What is Crisis

Crisis refers to sequence of unwanted events at the workplace which lead to disturbances and major unrest amongst the individuals.

Crisis generally arises on a short notice and triggers a feeling of threat and fear in the employees. In simpler words leads to uncertainty and causes major harm to the organization and its employees.

Communication in Crisis

Crisis communication is an initiative which aims at protecting the reputation of the organization and maintaining its public image.

  • 84% of organizations have emergency communication plan in place.
  • 55% use 3 or more emergency communication processes.

Crisis Communication Plan can Help In

  • Protect your employees and other stakeholders during a crisis
  • Prevent the spread of misinformation in the workplace
  • Align employees with the overall crisis management strategy and enable them to work towards the same goals
