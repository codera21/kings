What is Crisis

Crisis Communication

  • Communication from of company's spokesperson Internally or enternaly
  •  
  • To Protect Company's Brand and Reputation

Crisis Communication is Useful For

Protecting your employees and other stakeholders during a crisis.
Preventing the spread of misinformation in the workplace.
Aligning employees crisis management strategy and goals

Before

Do we have a crisis management plan?
Has our executive management team been adequately trained?
What’s our perception of the organization’s vulnerabilities and key risks?

During

Is it clear which members of the board will be responsible for what during a crisis?
Is the organization communicating appropriately and transparently to all stakeholders?
Do we know whom we would call upon?

After

Do we require an independent review in the wake of a crisis so that lessons may be learned?

General Layout of a Communication Plan

  1. Introduction
  2. Contacts
  3. Potential Disasters
  4. Action Priorities
  5. Media Relations
  6. Physical and organizational Provisions
  7. Post-Crisis Planing
