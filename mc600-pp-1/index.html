What is Crisis

Communication in Crisis

Crisis communication is an initiative which aims at protecting the reputation of the organization and maintaining its public image.

Crisis Communication in can Help in

  • Protect your employees and other stakeholders during a crisis
  • Prevent the spread of misinformation in the workplace
  • Align employees with the overall crisis management strategy and enable them to work towards the same goals

Facts

  • 84% of organizations have an emergency communication plan in place
  • 55% of organizations have 3 or more emergency communication processes

  • 49% have been engaged in crisis preparedness.
  • 32% have engaged in crisis simulations or trainings

General Layout of a Communication Plan.

  1. Introduction
  2. Contacts
  3. Potential Disasters
  4. Action Priorities
  5. Media Relations
  6. Physical and organizational Provisions
  7. Post-Crisis Planing
