What is Crisis

Crisis refers to sequence of unwanted events at the workplace which lead to disturbances and major unrest amongst the individuals.

Crisis generally arises on a short notice and triggers a feeling of threat and fear in the employees. In simpler words crisis leads to uncertainty and causes major harm to the organization and its employees.
